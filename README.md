TypeNova_ 🚀
Elevate your keystrokes. Master your speed.

Welcome to TypeNova_, a modern, feature-rich typing platform designed to help users test, track, and dramatically improve their typing skills. Whether you're a casual typist looking to break the 60 WPM barrier, a competitive player wanting to race friends, or a developer mastering complex syntax, TypeNova_ adapts to your exact goals.

Built with performance and user experience in mind, this platform goes beyond standard typing tests by introducing deep analytics, custom modes, and an engaging progression system.

✨ Key Features
Real-Time Analytics Engine: Get instant, granular feedback on your WPM (Words Per Minute), raw keystroke speed, and accuracy percentages.

Developer Mode (Code Snippets): Tired of typing standard prose? Switch to Developer Mode to practice syntax-heavy coding challenges, featuring extensive snippet libraries for C programming, Python, and web frameworks.

Multiplayer Arena: Challenge your friends in real-time typing races. Spin up a custom lobby, invite your crew, and see who dominates the global leaderboard.

Gamified Progression System: Earn XP, level up your profile, and unlock custom terminal-inspired themes, sound packs, and animated carets as you hit new milestones.

Dynamic Heatmap Diagnostics: Identify your weak spots with a personalized keyboard heatmap that highlights frequently missed keys and slow finger transitions.

Zen Mode: A minimalist, distraction-free UI that fades out everything except the current word you are typing.
